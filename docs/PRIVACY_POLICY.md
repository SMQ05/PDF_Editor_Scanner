# Privacy Policy

**PDF Scanner App**  
*Last Updated: December 2024*

## Overview

PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information Collection

### Camera Access
- The app requires camera access to scan documents
- Camera feed is processed locally on your device
- No camera images are transmitted to external servers

### Document Storage
- Scanned documents are stored in app-private storage on your device
- Documents are not uploaded to cloud services
- You maintain full control over your documents

### Metadata Handling
- **EXIF Stripping**: Location data (GPS coordinates), device information, and other metadata are automatically removed from scanned images before PDF creation
- **PDF Metadata**: Generated PDFs contain minimal neutral metadata (no personal identifiers)

### Analytics & Advertising

#### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

#### In-App Purchases
When you make a purchase:
- Transactions are processed by Google Play
- We receive purchase confirmation, not payment details
- See [Google Play's Terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) for details

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Document contents
- Specific location data
- Contacts or personal files
- Account credentials
- Biometric data

## Data Storage

- All documents remain on your device
- App settings stored locally via SQLite database
- No cloud backup or sync features

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Retention

- App settings: Until app uninstall

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete any scanned documents
- Clear app data via Android Settings
- Opt out of personalized ads via Android Settings > Google > Ads
- Uninstall the app at any time

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures:
- App-private storage (inaccessible to other apps)
- Automatic metadata stripping
- Local-only processing
